Credit Control gives you the tools to challenge anything on your credit reports you believe to be inaccurate, incomplete, or unverifiable according to the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). We compare the creditre portdatabe tween all 3 bureaus to determinep otential errors on your credit reports and, by default, will include these potential errors as dispute reasons in the letters.

The "StartDisputes" feature allows you to schedule disputes in advance to the 3 major credit bureaus and to all creditors/collection agencies involved all with just one-click.

Although we have default settings that do most of the hard work for you, feel free to customize the disputes as you see fit.

Clicking "StartDisputes" means you accept all settings and consent to begin the dispute process.

You can stop disputes any time by clicking "Stop Disputes".
